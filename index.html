AEO Powersports is proud to be the one of the premier destinations in the southwest for Suspension tuning and re-valving. About Us

After the opening of AEO Powersports in Murrieta, CA in June 2019, we teamed up with former factory WP Suspension tech Mark Johnson - also know as Research Engineering Performance, to be our head suspension tech and tuner but also to broaden our reach and services around the nation. All products made by R.E.P. are offered at all AEO Powersports locations for purchase.
